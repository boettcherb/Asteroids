ASTEROIDS is a space-themed 2D shooter arcade game developed
by Atari in 1979. The object of the game is to stay alive as long
as possible while avoiding asteroids and UFOs. The player starts
with three lives and receives an extra life every 10,000 points.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Original Game Designers: Lyle Rains, Ed Logg, Dominic Walsh
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Controls:
    UP ARROW: Accelerate
    LEFT ARROW: Turn Left
    RIGHT ARROW: Turn Right
    SPACEBAR: Shoot
    ESCAPE: Exit Game
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Points:
    Large Asteroid: 20
    Medium Asteroid: 50
    Small Asteroid: 100
    Large UFO: 200
    Small UFO: 1000
